# school-data-power-bi-project
# 🏫 National School Data Dashboard Analysis

An end-to-end Business Intelligence (BI) and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project analyzing national educational landscapes, workforce sector distributions, and student demographics. This repository contains interactive **Power BI** dashboards, custom data structures, and an executive presentation summarizing key operational insights across educational sectors and states.

## 📌 Project Overview
The objective of this analysis is to evaluate national educational structures to understand demographic balancing and workforce deployment. By transforming raw institutional logs into a structured visual framework, this project examines total student enrollment split by academic levels, gender parity ratios, and staffing concentrations to provide data-driven visibility into school systems.

---

## 📊 Core Business & Educational Insights

The analytical dashboard tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) across several structural pillars:

### 💼 1. Workforce Sector Distribution
* **Government Dominance:** The educational workforce is heavily concentrated within the Government sector, representing nearly two-thirds of the total national educational staff at **63.15% (3.06M staff members)**.
* **Private & Faith-Based Sectors:** Non-government workforce footprints are evenly distributed between the **Catholic sector at 18.56% (0.9M)** and the **Independent sector at 18.29% (0.89M)**.

### 🎒 2. Student Enrollment & Levels
* **Primary vs. Secondary Split:** National enrollment matrices reflect a larger institutional base in lower education, tracking **43M Total Students at the Primary Level** compared to **31M Total Students at the Secondary Level**.

### ⚖️ 3. Gender Parity Analysis
* **Balanced Parity:** Strict gender equity is maintained seamlessly across both active educational tiers with a near 50/50 distribution split:
  * **Primary Tier:** 22M Male students vs. 21M Female students.
  * **Secondary Tier:** 16M Male students vs. 15M Female students.

### 📍 4. State-Level Enrollment Concentration (Top Regions)
* **New South Wales (NSW):** Leads national enrollment figures by a wide margin, tracking **20.27M combined students** (15.27M Government Sector / 4.99M Catholic Sector).
* **Victoria:** Represents the second-largest educational hub with **15.38M combined students** (11.36M Government / 4.01M Catholic).
* **Queensland:** Follows closely as a major regional driver with **13.15M combined students** (10.34M Government / 2.80M Catholic).
